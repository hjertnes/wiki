This is the latest Ryan Holiday book as of writing this. His stuff are mostly good, but not great. I do like most of them though. I liked this book a lot. It is a good introduction if you want to get a more historical perspective to stoicism. I would not start with it, but it is a good read once you have heard about Auerlius and Seneca and are confused.
