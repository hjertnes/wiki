I was amazed when I saw this movie for the first time. It was like nothing I have ever seen before for the 16 year old me. And it is still nothing like anything else.

This movie has a lot of stuff I really enjoy. The really gritty almost comic book like style is freaking awesome. I also love how it is out of order. And the story isn't like a Marvel comic, but still not like a realistic movie.
