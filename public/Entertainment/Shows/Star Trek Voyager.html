I have recently gotten really into Star Trek. It started when I watched Discovery. And I'm kind of working my way through it all in a weird and random fashion.

The Voyager series wasn't that great. I loved the first season, then it got kind of boring, and picked up again when seven of seas joined. And then it got kind of stale again during that season. It wasn't bad, just felt very repetitive. And not that interesting.
