I finally bought one. I've had it on my list since I first heard about it in 2014 or something.

It is awesome. I love it. One of the great things about working from home is that I can fidget with it and keep focus in the meeting I'm in without annoying everyone.

I guess a lot of people don't get that. But for some of us, doing stuff like doodling, or fidgeting with something improves our ability to focus on meetings
