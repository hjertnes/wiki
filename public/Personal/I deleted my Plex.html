I deleted my Plex

  • Notes
  • Personal

I used to have a very large Plex library, with a lot of movies and TV shows etc. It was close to 5TB. It became a mayjor pain in the ass to manage. Both to back up, find disk setups it worked well with etc. So at some point I just deleted all of the stuff that wasn't unwatched. And moved it to the system drive of my MacBook.

Suddenly it was all much more fun again.

I never keep stuff around after I've watched it. Much easier to manage.
