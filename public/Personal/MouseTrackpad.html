Using a trackpad and a mouse

  • Notes
  • Personal

On my desk, both at work and at home I use both a Magic Trackpad and a mouse. Not at the same time, but I have both. This might be weird to a lot of people, but I think it is really useful to have both. A mouse is a lot better at moving things far and fast. While a trackpad is much better at gestures.
