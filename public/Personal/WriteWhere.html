I have this place and I have my blog. What goes where? Most of the time I think it's obvious what goes where. But sometimes it is not. In general the rules are that:

  • The blog contains things that matters now but probably don't matter later
  • It also has a few pages that might belong here. But are on the blog because they feel more at home there.
  • This wiki is for small or large texts that are linked together with links and backlinks. Where the intent is to update things as time goes by
  • The blog has a rss feed this wiki has not
  • The blog is a stream of "updates" while this is more like a huge document
