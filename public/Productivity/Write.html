Most of my writing is done in a weird way.

  1. When I get an idea I write it down in a notebook or in drafts.
  2. Later it is sorted and added to OmniFocus
  3. Then I draft it on paper
  4. At a given interval(usually 50 pages) I transcribe the ones that is good enough to a text file. The rest are re-added as ideas to omnifocus.
  5. Then I look over all of them and move it to my blog or my org-roam.

It is a very slow process, and sometimes I goes months between writing it and it going anywhere online.
