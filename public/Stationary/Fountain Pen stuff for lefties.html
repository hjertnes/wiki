Fountain Pen stuff for lefties

  • Notes
  • Stationary

It is no need to not go into fountain pens, just because you are left handed. There are a lot of things you can do to limit the problems of smuding. And with some training you can learn some techniques that limit the need for being limited to stuff with short dry time.

  • LT1917: great paper that in my impression have among the fastest dry times.
  • Noodlers Bernanke: I hate the owner. But this ink dries more or less immediatly
  • Finer nibs.

I still use LT1917, but I have never been that into finer nibs. And I mostly just use Iroshizuku inks.

But I recommend making it as easy as possible in the beginning. And then slowly go nuts as you can used to things.
