On why I prefer Iroshizuku

  • Notes
  • Stationary

My go to ink is the Pilot Iroshizuku, I have tried a bunch of different inks over the years, but at a point I just reached the conclusion that Iroshizuku is what works the best for me. Here is why:

  • It dries fast
  • Has good flow (the pens starts writing fast)
  • Colours are good

For me, Iroshizuku is the compromise that works the best.
