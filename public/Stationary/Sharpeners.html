The sharpeners I use

  • Classroom Friendly: the results are good, but it is noisy and leaves shavings all over
  • KUM Longpoint: the results are okay, but too short for my taste, it's plastic and cheap feeling
  • KUM Masterpiece: the results are okay, but too short for my taste, it is built well.
  • Pollux: the results are great, but the tip breaks too easy and gets stuck.
