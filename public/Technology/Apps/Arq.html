  • macOS Arq is the backup app I have been using for years. It is a cloud backup solution. I have used it a number of times for restoring smaller stuff, and it have worked perfectly well for me.

It is a little bit weird. It isn't a service, but rather just the backup app. You pay a significant amount of money for the app and then you pick a backup service in addition.
