Foodnoms are the calorie tracker I am using. It was the main thing that made it possible for me to loose 17 KG in 2020, and I'm not using it to continue in the same diraction in 2021. It is a privacy respecting calorie tracking app.

Unlike the other ones I have used it is just a calorie tracker. Doesn't try to sell me on diets or any other crap.
