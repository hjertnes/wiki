Overcast have been my go to app for listening to podcasts since it came out. Other than some short attempts at other apps. I have used it all the time. And I think it is great.

Unlike all the podcast apps I used prior to it, I love it. It reminds me about old school Mac apps. The UI isn't flashy. It is plain, looks good and works great.
