Plantronics Backbeat Fit 3200

  • Notes

I recently got these for running and other things where I want something lighter than my large headphones. So long so good. They're comfortable, the sound is good for what they are and they don't fall out when I'm running. My AirPods never fell out either, but it always felt like they almost did. And the sound in these are much better than the AirPods. I also love that the case let you click them into a slot instead of just dropping them in. I always had problems getting the AirPods to charge properly.
