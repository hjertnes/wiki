I didn't get it on my old MacBook. I'll most likely continue to use that on though. Now that they have done some changes to the layout of if, I don't think it is much of a problem. You have a physical button for Esc, and the TouchID area is divided from the rest. So you have two small buttons, and the area between them is a screen. As far as I can see, there are two problems with how Apple does this:

  • They should sell a external version of it
  • It should be optional.

Anyways. I quite like it. I installed BetterTouchTool and have configured it to show an emoji picker, battery status and the currently playing song. Other than that I like how accessible actions I do a lot like media controls, volume control, locking screen and mission control. I think this is something that could get a lot better if Apple put more love into it.
