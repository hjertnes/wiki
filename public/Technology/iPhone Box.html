When I got the iPhone 12 I was delighted to discover some stuff.

  • USB-C cable
  • No useless powerplug
  • No useless headphones

I think it is a great move. I have no idea how many of them I have thrown out over the years. They might have been useful once upon a time. But the power plugs became useless in the iPhone 6 era, and the headphones got useless when the jack went away.
